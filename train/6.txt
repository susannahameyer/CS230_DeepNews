Updated at 10:10 p.m. Monday : Revised to include injury to two North Texans and State Rep. Armando Martinez . Despite warnings about the dangers and consequences of celebratory gunfire , Dallas police received more than 700 calls about random gunshots as residents rang in the new year . The 711 gunfire calls occurred between 10 p.m. New Year 's Eve and 2 a.m. Jan. 1 , with the majority coming between midnight and 2 a.m. Police also received 282 fireworks calls . Police also arrested 16 people on suspicion of driving while intoxicated . Last year , police got more than 900 calls between 8 p.m. and 2 a.m. about shots fired and fireworks .
